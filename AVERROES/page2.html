What Our Students Fight For

A declaration of our values and faith, our education is purposeful and directed. With extensive knowledge, understanding, and experience, we do so to realize these values infusing our school community with hope and possibility.


What Our Students Fight Against

Above all, our education is about creating positive community change locally, nationally, and globally. We continually strive to position Averroes as a pillar of our community, a school welcome to all with opportunities for all.

We begin with an understanding that the system of human equality is broken, yet we are empowered to address the challenges from racism to poverty to climate change, seeking to support those oppressed. With social innovation and the power of social media, we pledge solutions for the thousands of voiceless victims worldwide. In doing so, we continue to develop a deep understanding of the challenges facing other countries. We seek partnerships with institutions, corporations, foundations, and community organization committed to positive change around the world. We advocate, use our privilege, our voices, protest, petition and act with greater and greater passion, effort, and purpose. Staying true to what we believe in, we believe change begins with us.

To achieve our aims, we pledge to be respectful, listen, and communicate without judgment. We treat each other as equals, offer fairness, and understanding. We refuse to blame but forgive to collaborate as we work for change. Generous and sensitive, we offer companionship, loyalty, and emotional support especially in this time of need. We strive to help one other with honesty, lightheartedness, and without drama.

We commit to honoring the Constitution and will uphold its principles in creating positive change for our school, our community, our world.
