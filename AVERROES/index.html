Our Educational Model

a Three-Pronged Approach Carefully Designed

1

Academics

Averroes High School is dedicated to developing well-informed, academic, responsible, compassionate, and collaborate young adults.

2

Enrichment

We build the community of learners. We support the enrichment of each student on an individual level as well as a community level. 

3

Environment

We provide a space where Muslims and people of faith can come together in a safe space and feel confident in their belief and their practices.

ACADEMICS

Academics are about the study of sciences. At Averroes, we take our academics seriously by offering a wide range of classes for our students. We continuously work to integrate the sciences in our curriculum and overlap relevancy for students in all the areas.

More on Academics

ENRICHMENT

Another major component to the Averroes Model is student enrichment. This is everything else we offer as a school to build the ideal community for learners. We support the enrichment of each student on an individual level and a community level.

More on Enrichment

ENVIRONMENT

Averroes aims to develop a sound learning environment for our students through the following five components: Islamic Compass, Social Emotional Learning, Restorative Justice, Parent Education Training, and Constructive Learning Environment.
